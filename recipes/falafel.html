Ingredients

  • Dried chickpeas
  • Fresh herbs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Kosher Salt
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, and a little cayenne pepper
  • Baking powder
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas for 24 hours.
  2. Make mixture
  3. Refrigerate (important.)
  4. Form patties or balls
  5. Fry

Carefully drop the falafel in the oil, using a slotted spoon, and fry for 3-5 minutes until medium brown on the outside. Avoid over-crowding the falafel; fry them in batches if necessary.
